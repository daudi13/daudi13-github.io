Who I am

Frontend developer based in Nairobi

I am a Frontend developer born and raised in Nairobi, for as long as I can remeber I always wanted to be involved with tech, Tech has always sparked something in me be it creative apps, colorful websites,sleek computers or anything that looks so simple yet very sophisticated in its creation.And to me becoming a software developer was a career that went in tandem with all those things.

I've always been an avid problem solver and having grown in the era of the startups and the big tech companies it was only a matter of time before I decided to be part of the revolution. My school has been the internet, I was 25 years old when I came to the realization that you didn't even need to step in a computer science class to become a tech

My main programming language is JavaScript but being a developer makes everyday an opportunity to learn something new. when I'm not on the computer I'm painting something on a canvas or just taking a walk enjoying life.
